About Us

Creativity in Design, Reliability in Delivery

Joweb Graphic Designers Limited is a registered company in Kenya, founded in 2006 to meet the growing demand for creative advertising, branding, and promotional materials in both local and international markets.

Over the years, we have grown into a leading creative design and logistics partner in East Africa — offering graphic design, branding, printing, transport & logistics, and academic writing services.

Our Mission

To provide Creative, Innovative, Print, Brand, Academic Writing, and Logistics Services that strive to meet client expectations — on time, every time.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s leading provider of innovative, impactful design, branding, academic writing, and logistics solutions.

Our Principles

  • Integrity & Fairness – Remaining true to honesty and fairness.
  • Innovation – Delivering forward-thinking solutions that drive impact.
  • Quality & Excellence – Ensuring the highest standards in everything we produce.
  • Responsibility – Balancing business goals with community contribution.

Customer Service

Our customer service desk is staffed with trained professionals who attend to every client need with efficiency and courtesy. We aim to serve promptly, build trust, and maintain long-lasting relationships.

Quality Control

We are committed to strict quality assurance, ensuring all our services and products meet international standards, including KEBS compliance.

Financial Capacity

We are equipped to handle projects of varying scales, with the capacity to service orders of up to KES 500 million at any given time.
